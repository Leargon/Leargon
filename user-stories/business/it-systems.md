#### USER STORY 'IT system inventory'
**AS A** company\
**IF** \
**I WANT** to maintain an inventory of all IT systems used in the organisation\
**SO THAT** we can document which systems support which processes and identify the responsible teams

#### USER STORY 'Actualisation'
**AS AN** admin\
**IF** \
**I WANT** to administer an IT system's name, type, vendor, and URL\
**SO THAT** all information about the system is up-to-date and accurate

#### USER STORY 'Link to processes'
**AS AN** admin or process owner\
**IF** \
**I WANT** to link IT systems to the business processes they support\
**SO THAT** the relationship between technology and business operations is visible and traceable in the processing register
