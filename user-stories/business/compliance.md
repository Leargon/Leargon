#### USER STORY 'Processing register'
**AS A** company\
**IF** \
**I WANT** to document all personal data processing activities with their legal basis, purpose, data categories, responsible parties, and processors\
**SO THAT** we comply with the record-keeping requirements of GDPR Art. 30 and Swiss DSG Art. 12

#### USER STORY 'Legal basis'
**AS A** privacy officer or DPO\
**IF** \
**I WANT** to assign a legal basis to each processing activity (Consent, Contract, Legal Obligation, Vital Interests, Public Task, Legitimate Interest)\
**SO THAT** every personal data processing activity has a documented and lawful justification

#### USER STORY 'Data processors'
**AS A** privacy officer or DPO\
**IF** third-party vendors process personal data on our behalf\
**I WANT** to maintain an inventory of data processors, track DPA status, and link them to the relevant processing activities\
**SO THAT** our processor relationships are documented and contractual obligations under GDPR Art. 28 and DSG Art. 9 are traceable

#### USER STORY 'Cross-border transfers'
**AS A** privacy officer or DPO\
**IF** personal data is transferred outside Switzerland or the EU/EEA\
**I WANT** to document the destination country and the applicable transfer safeguard for each transfer\
**SO THAT** cross-border transfer obligations under DSG Art. 16 and GDPR Art. 44 are met

#### USER STORY 'Data Protection Impact Assessment'
**AS A** privacy officer or DPO\
**IF** a processing activity poses a high risk to data subjects\
**I WANT** to trigger and document a DPIA, including identified risks, safeguards, and residual risk rating\
**SO THAT** we comply with the DPIA requirements of GDPR Art. 35 and DSG Art. 22 before the processing begins

#### USER STORY 'Compliance exports'
**AS A** privacy officer or DPO\
**IF** \
**I WANT** to export the processing register, data processor list, and DPIA register as CSV files\
**SO THAT** I can submit audit-ready records to supervisory authorities or use them in external compliance tools
