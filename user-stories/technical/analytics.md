#### USER STORY 'View personal dashboard'
**AS A** logged in user\
**IF** \
**I WANT** to see a personal home screen showing my owned entities and processes grouped by type, items that need my attention (missing legal basis, open DPIA, missing owner), and a feed of recent catalogue activity\
**SO THAT** I have an immediate overview of my governance responsibilities and the latest changes in the catalogue

#### USER STORY 'View governance maturity overview'
**AS AN** admin\
**IF** \
**I WANT** to see a governance maturity overview on the home screen showing percentage coverage for entity ownership, process compliance, classification coverage, domain structure, DPIA coverage, process-unit assignment, and data processor documentation\
**SO THAT** I can assess the organisation's overall governance health at a glance and identify areas that need attention

#### USER STORY 'View team insights'
**AS AN** admin\
**IF** \
**I WANT** to view team-level analytics showing which organisational units own the most entities and processes, which units have the highest compliance gaps, and how process ownership is distributed across teams\
**SO THAT** I can identify overloaded teams, governance bottlenecks, and units with disproportionate ownership responsibility
